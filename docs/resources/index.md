# Resources Index

We complied a list of resources related to various aspects of pursuring a variety of careers outside of tenure-track academic jobs. These include values exercises to help you determine what kinds of jobs might be a good fit and to help build resumes/CVs, papers and discussions on career diversity practices, resource hubs compiled by other institutions, and more.
