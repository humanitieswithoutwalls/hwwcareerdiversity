# Timeline

<details>
  <summary>Applying to PhD Programs</summary>
  Think about what matters to you: in addition to your research interests and areas, think about issues and causes that are important to you, the kind of lifestyle you need to be healthy, etc. Choose an advisor carefully. Talk to current students. Dont just look at the specific program you're applying to, but consider the university as a whole. What kinds of research centers are on campus and what do they offer? What kinds of relationships does the university have with local communities? Where do you want to be involved?
  <summary>Starting Your PhD Program</summary>
  Find mentors (not just your advisor). Look for researchers and scholars who are doing work in the areas you're interested in, both in and outside your department.
  <summary>Coursework</summary>
  Cultivate relationships, make summers meaningful, identify skills to develop
